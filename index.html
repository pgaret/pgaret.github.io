About Me


Hi there, welcome to my portfolio. My name is Peregrin Garet- nice to meet you. I write code, technical documents, stories, and more.

I graduated in 2015 from RPI, an engineering school in upstate NY, with a dual major in Business Management and Game Development. While I was at school, I divided my work experience between code, project management, and game design. I also took a number of writing courses.

I began work immediately after graduation as an Analyst at Vitech Systems Group, an IT firm providing holistic browser-based software solutions for pension, insurance, and investment companies. At Vitech, I am responsible for documenting software updates and changes, and ensuring they are executed correctly, both from the front end and the back end.
